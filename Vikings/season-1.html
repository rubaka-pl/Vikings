The series introduces us to the harsh and mysterious world of Ragnar Lothbrok, a Viking warrior and farmer who thirsts to explore—and raid—the distant shores of the ocean. His ambition puts him at odds with the local chieftain, Earl Haraldson, who insists on sending his raiders to the poor East rather than the uncharted West. When Ragnar teams up with his boat builder friend, Floki, to create a new generation of fearless ships capable of conquering the turbulent northern seas, the stage is set for conflict.

But despite all the warfare and bloodshed, the Vikings are also a story of family and brotherhood, capturing the love and loyalty between Ragnar and his wife, Lagertha, a fierce warrior in her own right. It's a tale of Ragnar's brother, Rollo, a fierce fighter consumed by jealousy; of Earl Haraldson's wife, Siggy, a compliant beauty who may not be entirely faithful; and of the monk Athelstan, whose Christian morals clash with the pagan Viking society. As ambitions and innovations shake civilization, these characters will be tested—and their way of life will never be the same again.
